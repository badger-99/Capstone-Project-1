DIGITAL CREATIVES GLOBAL SUMMIT 2023

Every 3 years, the Digital Creatives summit brings together a community of creators from various disciplines and backgrounds that use technology as the primary tool in implementing and sharing their ideas and visions. This year, we want to expand out invitation to organizations and individuals that want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of free cultural expression and open knowledge geared towards creating a shared pool of intelectual assets. So if you're active and engaged in the world of open content creation and knowledge sharing - Free software supporters, Wikipedia activists, Open Knowledge Foundation activists, art galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments, foundations, lawyers, social activists, and others- we hope you will consider joining us in order to help create a thriving ecosystem.

Please contact up for any firther questions about the DC Global Summit 2023 throug email at dcsummit2023@dcinternational.org

Digital Creatives Logo

Because we are a multinational, multicultural community, the logo for the Digital Creatives organization was designed with a minimalistic approach in mind. The aim was to have a logo that anyone can Identify with despite their place of origin, background, or culture.
